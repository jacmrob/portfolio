These courses were taught to high school students as part of North Shore's Early College Program. All curricula and lecture slides were developed and created by me.
CPS 100: IT and Its Applications
Students learn the basics of computer software and hardware, what applications are and how they work in modern ecosystems, networking and the Internet, how to work with databases (including basic SQL). Students use Microsoft Word, Powerpoint, Excel, and Access to complete projects related to course concepts.
